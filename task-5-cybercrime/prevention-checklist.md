# Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)

## 🔐 General Online Safety
- Use **strong, unique passwords** for each account (minimum 12 characters, mix of letters, numbers, symbols).
- Enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** on email, banking, and social media accounts.
- Do not click on **unknown links** or download attachments from suspicious emails/messages.
- Always verify website URLs (look for **https://** and correct domain names).
- Avoid using **public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions** like banking or login.
- Keep your devices updated with the latest **security patches and antivirus software**.
- Limit personal information shared on social media (phone number, address, college ID).
- Log out from accounts when using shared or public computers.

## 💰 Financial / UPI Safety
- Never share your **UPI PIN, OTP, or banking credentials** with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank.
- Do not accept **“collect requests”** on UPI apps from unknown users.
- Always verify **QR codes** before scanning—fraudsters may tamper with them.
- Use only official UPI apps like **BHIM, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm** from trusted app stores.

## 🚨 If Something Goes Wrong
- Immediately report cyber fraud at:
  - 🌐 National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
  - 📞 Helpline Number: 1930
- Inform your bank immediately in case of financial fraud.
